About

I lead a team dedicated to providing written, video, and interactive eLearning materials for customers and internal stakeholders of a leading enterprise SaaS company. We make complex information clear and compelling — delivering content that drives product adoption, guides customers and internal teams through implementation, and empowers them to get the most value out of the product after go-live.

My experience spans executive-level writing and video production, content strategy, AI workflows and automation, user experience research, instructional design, and team leadership. My current focus is on developing strong writers, building efficient AI-driven processes, and staying hands-on with our highest priority deliverables.

Before Veeva, I worked as a technical writer at Graco and conducted usability research at the University of Minnesota. I hold degrees in Technical Writing and Communications and English from the University of Minnesota, both summa cum laude.
